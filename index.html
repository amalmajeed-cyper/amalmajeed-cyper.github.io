How We Work

The Forgex Formula

01

Creative Storyboarding

We work with your culinary team to align on your brand identity. We craft visual mood boards, custom lighting maps, and plate style guidelines prior to production day.

1
02

Styling & Setting

We sculpt scenes using atmospheric lighting profiles (chiaroscuro, bright airy, or neon-cocktail tones). We styles plates, mist ingredients, and craft micro-smoke vectors for ultimate visual texture.

2
03

Dynamic Shooting

Using ultra-high-resolution rigs, stabilizers, and high-speed focal lengths, we capture both macro details and wide room vibes. We catch chefs in action and the splash of fresh pours.

3
04

Cinematic Edit

Every frame is polished. We apply tailored gourmet color-grades, motion stabilization, precise sound design, and custom dimensions optimized for websites, print menus, or TikTok and Reels.
